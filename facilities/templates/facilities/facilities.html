Here at freedog we put your dog first

Safety

Both of our hire fields are enclosed by 6ft high dog-proof fencing. The fields are screened from each other to allow nervous or reactive dogs to play in peace. Our larger field can easily accomodate up to 5 dogs with ample space to run and play on a level grass surface. Each field has its own separate entrance and parking area.

Fun

With up to 3 dogs included in each booking (and 2 more can be added for a small extra fee) freedog is a great place to meet up with friends. Enjoy a coffee from our vending machine while you watch your dog play whilst seated in our undercover seating area, perfect for those rainy days.

Exercise

Test your dogs agility with our obstacle course keeping their minds and body active. We have a tunnel, ramp and see-saw to explore. Perfect for engaging training sessions. Alternatively allow them to run and play in our larger 3 acre field. They will soon be exhausted and happy and looking forward to their next visit.
